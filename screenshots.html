Typical working session of SpikeSort featuring spike waveforms panel, features panel, spike browser and the interpreter.

Spike Waveshapes panel shows waveshapes of all detected and clustered spikes.

Spike Features panel is a grid of two-dimensional projections (mosaic plot) of all features (here only projection on the first principal component and peak-to-peak amplitude is shown).

Spike Browser superimposes the identified spikes on the raw recordings allowing for quick visual validation of the sorting results. The four traces represent different contacts of the same tetrode.
